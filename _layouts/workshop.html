Who Should Attend?

This workshop is designed predominantly for people working as a product owner, service owner or product manager alongside Agile development teams. However, working in this kind of role is not a prerequisite, and the workshop will be valuable to anyone working in agile software development.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of our most frequently asked questions about our workshops:

How many people can attend this workshop?

All of our workshops are designed for groups of 2-8 people. If you're looking for more than 8 people to attend, we usually recommend splitting the group in half and running two or more separate sessions.

If you're looking for a 1:1 session, please contact us to arrange some private coaching.

Do you run this workshop as a public class?

Not at the moment, but please contact us if you're interested, and if we get enough demand from the same area, we'll arrange something.

Do you provide copies of course materials, such as slides, handouts, etc?

In order to reduce waste, we don't handout hard copies of course materials, but each attendee will be provided with a link to download everything they have used at the session.
